Reviewer Guidelines

Principles for fair, constructive, and timely peer review

Core Responsibilities

Reviewers are expected to assess originality, technical soundness, clarity, reproducibility, and relevance. All reviews should be respectful, evidence-based, and actionable for authors and editors.

Review Expectations

  • Accept invitations only when the manuscript matches your expertise.
  • Declare conflicts of interest immediately.
  • Keep all manuscript content confidential.
  • Provide comments for both authors and editors.
  • Submit decisions within the stated deadline.

Decision Guidance

  • Accept: Ready for publication with only minor editorial changes.
  • Minor Revision: Strong paper needing limited clarification or formatting updates.
  • Major Revision: Promising work requiring substantive methodological or presentation changes.
  • Reject: Insufficient novelty, major methodological flaws, or poor fit for scope.
Reviewer Checklist
  • Originality assessed
  • Methods verified
  • References checked
  • Ethical concerns flagged
  • Decision justified clearly
