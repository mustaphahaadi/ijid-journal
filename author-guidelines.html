Author Guidelines

Submission standards and manuscript preparation requirements

Before You Submit

  • Ensure the manuscript is original, unpublished, and not under consideration elsewhere.
  • Prepare a structured abstract, 4 to 6 keywords, and complete author affiliations.
  • Use clear sectioning for introduction, methods, results, discussion, and conclusion where applicable.
  • Confirm all figures, tables, and supplementary files are cited in the text.

Manuscript Format

IJID accepts original research, review papers, short communications, and technical reports. Manuscripts should be submitted in PDF, DOCX, or LaTeX-exported PDF. Recommended structure:

  • Title page with author names, affiliations, ORCID, and corresponding author email.
  • Abstract of 250 to 350 words and 4 to 6 keywords.
  • Main text with numbered headings and complete references.
  • Funding, data availability, acknowledgments, and conflict of interest statements.

Figures, Tables, and References

All visual elements must be high resolution and submitted in editable or publication-ready form. References should be complete, recent, and formatted consistently. Authors are responsible for permissions for any third-party material.

Submission Checklist

  • Cover letter prepared
  • Keywords included
  • All co-authors approved
  • Ethics statement included
  • References verified
  • Figures numbered correctly
  • Data statement added
  • Supplementary files named clearly
Quick Facts
  • Article types: Research, Review, Short Communication
  • Abstract length: 250 to 350 words
  • Keywords: 4 to 6
  • Review model: Double-blind
Need Help?

If you are unsure whether your manuscript fits IJID, contact the editorial office before submission.
