Frequently Asked Questions
How long does the review process take?

Our average review turnaround time is 21 days from submission to first decision. Complex papers may require additional time.

Is there an Article Processing Charge (APC)?

IJID is currently free for authors. There are no submission fees, processing charges, or publication costs.

Can I track my submission status?

Yes! Log in to your author dashboard to view real-time status updates, reviewer feedback, and any required actions for your submission.

How do I become a reviewer?

Register an account and select "Reviewer" as your role. Our editorial team will review your profile and expertise before activation.
