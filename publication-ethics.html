Publication Ethics

COPE-aligned integrity standards for authors, reviewers, and editors

Author Responsibilities

  • Submit only original work with accurate authorship attribution.
  • Disclose funding, conflicts of interest, and ethics approvals where required.
  • Avoid plagiarism, duplicate publication, image manipulation, and data fabrication.

Reviewer Responsibilities

  • Maintain confidentiality and impartiality.
  • Declare conflicts of interest before accepting a review.
  • Provide objective, substantiated, and respectful feedback.

Editorial Responsibilities

Editors evaluate submissions fairly and independently of author nationality, institutional affiliation, gender, or ideology. Suspected misconduct is investigated using COPE guidance, and corrections, retractions, or expressions of concern are issued when necessary.

Misconduct Examples
  • Plagiarism
  • Duplicate submission
  • Data fabrication
  • Undisclosed AI-generated content misuse
  • Peer review manipulation
