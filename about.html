About IJID

International Journal of Innovative Discoveries

Our Mission

The International Journal of Innovative Discoveries (IJID) is a peer-reviewed, open-access scholarly journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research across all areas of computer science, information technology, and interdisciplinary computing. Our mission is to foster the dissemination of knowledge, promote rigorous scientific inquiry, and bridge the gap between theoretical innovation and practical application.

Founded with the vision of making cutting-edge research accessible to the global academic community, IJID provides a platform for researchers, practitioners, and scholars from around the world to share their findings and contribute to the advancement of technology and science.

Scope & Coverage

IJID publishes original research articles, review papers, short communications, case studies, and technical reports in the following areas (but not limited to):

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Computer Vision & Image Processing
  • Data Science & Big Data Analytics
  • Cybersecurity & Privacy
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Blockchain & Distributed Systems
  • Quantum Computing
  • Software Engineering
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Cloud & Edge Computing
  • Robotics & Autonomous Systems

Open Access Policy

IJID is a fully open-access journal. All published articles are freely available to read, download, and share without any subscription or paywall. We believe that open access maximizes the impact and visibility of research, enabling broader dissemination and faster scientific progress. All articles are published under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) license.

Peer Review Process

IJID employs a rigorous double-blind peer review process. Every submitted manuscript undergoes initial screening by the editorial team, followed by evaluation by at least two independent expert reviewers in the relevant field. Our review process evaluates originality, methodology, technical soundness, clarity of presentation, and significance of contribution.

1. Submission

Authors submit manuscripts through our online portal

2. Peer Review

Double-blind review by 2+ expert reviewers

3. Publication

Accepted papers published with DOI within 48 hours

Publication Ethics

IJID adheres to the highest standards of publication ethics as outlined by the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE). We have zero tolerance for plagiarism, data fabrication, duplicate submissions, and any form of research misconduct. All submitted manuscripts are screened using plagiarism detection software before review.
